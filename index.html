What It Is

BreakLine sits on top of a modular crypto trading engine.

The platform combines technical strategies, feature-driven models, optional reinforcement learning, risk gating, execution safety, portfolio state tracking, and operations tooling. It is designed for technical buyers who want a working base they can extend, refine, or commercialize.

FAQ

Important context

Is this a guaranteed-profit product?

No. It is a trading software platform with real strategy, execution, and monitoring components. It is not a promise of returns.

Is it plug-and-play for retail users?

No. The current asset is best understood as a technical foundation for a product, business, or advanced operator.

Does it already support live execution?

Yes. The documented engine supports Kraken-based live or paper trading depending on the API environment.

Can it be extended into a subscription product?

Yes. The BreakLine app layer and platform wrapper were built to move in that direction, but production billing, compliance, and exchange-account onboarding are still product work.
